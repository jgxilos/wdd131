Conservation Matters

Venezuela ranks among the top 10 countries in bird diversity worldwide, with 1,426 species recorded, including 45 endemic species found nowhere else on Earth.

Our rich biodiversity is protected through national parks and conservation efforts, but many species face threats from habitat loss and climate change.
