Nest & Nature

Your local guide to birdwatching

1. Site Name

Nest & Nature

This name conveys warmth, approachability, and a deep connection with nature. It reflects the union between the “nest” (the home of birds) and “nature” (their living environment), offering a friendly and accessible experience for both beginners and experienced birdwatchers.

Suggested domain name (optional): nestandnature.org

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of Nest & Nature is to provide a reliable, accessible, and visually appealing resource for people who want to learn about and enjoy local birdwatching. The site will offer:

  • A guide to common bird species, including photos, descriptions, and habitats.
  • A curated list of the best local parks and natural areas for birdwatching.
  • A beginner-friendly section with essential tips, recommended gear, ideal seasons, and observation techniques.
  • A space that encourages appreciation for local biodiversity and fosters a meaningful connection with nature.

3. Scenarios

These are typical questions a site visitor might ask:

  1. “What bird species can I observe in my area, and how can I identify them?”
  2. “What are the best nearby parks or natural areas to start birdwatching?”
  3. “I’m a beginner—what basic equipment do I need to get started?”
  4. “During which time of year can I see the greatest variety of birds in my region?”

4. Color Schema

  • Forest Green (#2E5E3E): used for headings, navigation menus, and primary UI elements.
  • Bone White (#F9F7F1): used as the background color for cleanliness and readability.
  • Mustard Yellow (#E0A82E): used for buttons, icons, and interactive accents to convey energy and warmth.
  • Slate Gray (#444444): used for body text and secondary information to ensure elegant contrast and legibility.

This palette evokes nature, calmness, and vitality—perfectly aligned with the site’s theme.

5. Typography

  • Playfair Display: used for headings (H1, H2, H3) to convey elegance and an editorial, nature-inspired tone.
  • Lato: used for body text—clean, modern, and highly readable on screens.
  • Pacifico: used for slogans, quotes, or highlighted phrases to add a friendly, organic touch.
